LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Our web application is a comprehensive library management system that empowers users to explore, borrow, purchase, rate, and discuss a wide array of books. Users can effortlessly browse through our extensive collection. They have the flexibility to issue E-books, purchase e-books, rate their reads, and engage in discussions by leaving comments. With features like personalized profiles and convenient access controls, our platform ensures a seamless and enriching experience for all book enthusiasts.
